Best & Worst Weapons Reskin

Reskins guns based on Sluggy's excellent [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1911302566]Characters & Weapons Guide[/url].

Applies visual ranks using his suggestions:
[list]
[*][b]Worst[/b]: NERF-style barrel tip ─ Low ROI. Under-powered, outclassed, or too situational.
[*][b]Decent[/b]: No visual change ─ High ROI. Versatile and worth trying.
[*][b]Best[/b]: Gold plating ─ Best in class.
[/list]

[u][b]Notes[/b][/u]

Game version: 1.2.2

This mod is helpful if you're new to the game and don't know which guns to buy first. Once you've got a ton of stars it's worth experimenting a bit to find out what works best for you :)

Shield has no [b]best[/b] gun as only two of their pistols are [b]decent[/b]; a strong one and an armor-piercing one.
